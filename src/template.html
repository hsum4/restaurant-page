Who is Juno?

Juno is a brown-colored Shih-Tzu and the heart of our restaurant. Sweet, playful, and a little temperamental (especially when it's medicine time!), Juno represents the warmth and personality we bring into every meal. Proudly 100% Filipino, Juno inspires us to serve comforting food made with love and a touch of fun.
